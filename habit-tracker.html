What is tendto?

tendto is a simple tool for tracking tasks, habits, and recurring reminders — and noticing how you feel about them over time. Did that daily walk feel good today, or was it a slog? Over weeks, patterns emerge that help you decide what's worth keeping, what to adjust, and what to let go.

It works equally well for true habits (exercise, reading, sleep) and non-habitual reminders — things like calling a friend, changing your air filter, or scheduling a dentist visit.

What's a web app? tendto is a single web page — just an HTML file. There's nothing to install, no app store, no account to create. It runs entirely in your browser, which means your data never leaves your device. You can save the file itself to keep a permanent, offline copy that works forever even if this URL disappears. It looks and feels like an app, but it's just a file.

Getting started

Start by adding your first task — tap Tasks in the menu, then + Add Task. Not sure what to add? Here are a few ideas to mix habits with reminders:

🚶
Walk 20 minutes
Daily · duration · see if it improves your mood over time
📖
Read before bed
Daily · checkbox · replace doom-scrolling, track the streak
📞
Call a friend or family member
Every 14 days · you'll see it coming up even without push notifications
🌬️
Replace air filter
Every 90 days · the classic thing everyone forgets
💰
Review budget
Weekly (Sunday) · short and consistent beats thorough and never

One-time tasks are for things you want to remember to do once — not a habit, just a to-do with a date attached. They show up in your daily view when due and in Coming up in the days before. Once done or skipped, they stay in your history but never come back. Use them for:

📦
Return Amazon package
One-time · due tomorrow · disappears from Tasks once checked off
🏥
Follow up with doctor
One-time · stays on your list until you do it or decide to skip

Star ratings are how you check things off. Tap a star on any due task to mark it complete and rate it in one step — no separate checkbox. The ratings accumulate in Review so you can see over time which tasks feel worth it and which don't. Duration tasks (like a workout) show a time input once you've given a star.

💡 Tip: Add at least one daily habit. That way you'll open the app regularly and catch upcoming reminders before they slip by — no push notifications needed.

Your data stays yours

  • 🔒 Everything is stored only in this browser on this device. No account, no server, no one else sees it.
  • 🚫 No ads. No subscriptions. No tracking.
  • 🔔 No push notifications — by design. tendto is something you open, not something that interrupts you.
  • 💾 Save the HTML file to your device and it works forever, even offline. Future updates to this page won't affect your saved copy.

Add tendto to your home screen

If you're liking tendto and want it to feel more like a part of your phone, you can add it to your home screen. It will open like an app — no browser address bar, no clutter.

  • 📱 iPhone/iPad: tap the Share button (the box with an arrow) → Add to Home Screen
  • 🤖 Android: tap the browser menu (three dots) → Add to Home Screen

Note: if you add it to your home screen from a URL, you'll need an internet connection to open it. To use it fully offline, save the HTML file to your device first, then open it from your Files app and add that to your home screen.

About CSV export & import

The Export button downloads a .csv file — a plain text file containing all your habits and log entries. It's readable in any spreadsheet app and can be imported back into tendto on any device.

Why this matters: since your data lives only in this browser, if you clear your browser data or switch devices, it's gone — unless you have a CSV backup. Export regularly. Import to restore or sync between devices.

The CSV has two sections: --- HABITS --- (your habit definitions) and --- LOGS --- (every daily entry). Importing merges with existing data — duplicates are skipped automatically.
